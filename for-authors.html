The Problem

The Old Way Of
Selling Books Is Dead.

Every author knows this pattern. You spent real money hoping to move the book. You got a website that looks like everyone else's, fake reviews that Amazon eventually scrubbed, and promotion on accounts that don't exist. The book still sits.

Overseas Promo Trap

You paid a company a few hundred bucks to promote the book. They ran ads to bot profiles, stacked fake reviews, and handed you back a template site. Amazon flagged the reviews. Nothing moved.

Book Tour Ends. Traffic Dies.

No email capture. No reason for the reader to come back. Every launch starts from zero because nothing from the last one carries over. You're rebuilding an audience every time.

No Booking System

Podcast hosts, bookstore managers, and panel organizers want to book you. Right now they have to email, wait, cross-reference calendars, and usually give up. You lose appearances you never even heard about.

No Data. No Retargeting.

Every reader you meet at a signing, every YouTube viewer, every email list signup — scattered across platforms, untracked, untied together. You can't market to an audience you can't see.

The Platform

What An Author
Platform Actually Is.

Not a website. An infrastructure your whole career runs from. Every reading, podcast, launch, and conversation feeds back into one system that keeps selling — even when you're heads-down on the next book.

Author Website

Editorial-grade site built on your brand. Book pages, bio, press, blog. Mobile-first, fast, designed to convert a first-time visitor into an email subscriber.

Booking System

Hosts, venues, and organizers book you directly. Podcast appearances, readings, panels, workshops — all on a single calendar, no back-and-forth.

Email Capture & Drip

Every visitor has a reason to give you their email. Lead magnets, sample chapters, reader clubs. Automated welcome sequence drips them toward the buy.

Post-Purchase Sequence

The reader who just bought is the hottest lead you'll ever have. Thank-you, bonus material, review request, next-book pre-order — all fired automatically.

YouTube + Retargeting

Book trailers, chapter readings, cover reveals — optimized for search. Retargeting pixels fire on every view so your launch ad catches them again later.

Launch-Day Text Blast

Your network — friends, family, past readers, event contacts — gets a personal text on launch day. That first-day rank matters. This gets you there.

Press Kit

One URL to send every journalist, podcast, or festival curator. High-res headshots, bio, book covers, interview topics, contact info — one professional package.

Reader Club

A gated community or preview chapter that builds a core audience. The readers who opt in are the ones who pre-order book three before anyone else hears about it.

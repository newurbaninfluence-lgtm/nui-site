CONTACT FAQ

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes. Every project starts with a free strategy consultation where we discuss your business goals, target audience, competitive landscape, and branding needs. You'll receive a custom pricing estimate after the call with no obligation. Book directly at newurbaninfluence.com or call (248) 487-8747.

What are your business hours?

We're available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time. Email and voicemail are monitored outside business hours, and we respond within 24 hours on the next business day.

Do you work with clients outside Detroit?

Absolutely. While we're based in Detroit, we serve clients throughout Metro Detroit, Southeast Michigan, and nationwide. All consultations and project collaboration happen virtually, so location is never a barrier.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all emails and form submissions within 24 hours on business days. For immediate assistance, call us directly at (248) 487-8747 during business hours.

Our Story

How New Urban Influence Started

New Urban Influence was founded with a simple mission: to level the playing field for small businesses and startups. We saw talented entrepreneurs and innovative companies struggling to compete because they lacked professional branding and marketing strategies.

Too many small business owners were forced to choose between expensive agency fees they couldn't afford or settling for mediocre DIY solutions. We knew there had to be a better way.

So we built it. We created a model that delivers enterprise-quality branding and design at prices that make sense for growing businesses. Since our launch, we've helped dozens of Detroit-area companies transform their brands, attract better customers, and scale with confidence.

Why Detroit?

Detroit is more than our home—it's our inspiration. This city has always embodied reinvention, resilience, and bold creativity. We're proud to be part of Detroit's creative renaissance, helping local businesses and startups build the brands they deserve. From downtown to the suburbs, we're serving businesses across Michigan with the same energy and commitment to excellence.

Our Philosophy

How We Work

Strategy First, Design Second

We don't start with pretty graphics. Every project begins with a deep dive into your business, your audience, and your competitive landscape. We ask tough questions to understand what makes you unique, then we build a visual identity that communicates that difference.

Transparent Pricing, No Surprises

Our pricing is straightforward. Brand identity packages start at $1,500 and include everything you need: logo design, color systems, typography guidelines, social media templates, and comprehensive brand guidelines. You'll know exactly what you're paying for before we start.

Your Dedicated Client Portal

Throughout the project, you'll have access to a private client portal where you can review all assets, provide feedback in real-time, and download your completed brand materials. Transparency and collaboration are built into our process.

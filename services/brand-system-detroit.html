Frequently Asked Questions

BRAND SYSTEM FAQ

What exactly is included in Brand System?

Brand System includes four pillars: (1) Full brand identity — logo suite, color system, typography, brand guidelines, and social templates. (2) Custom multi-page website — responsive, SEO-optimized, with CMS. (3) Print collateral — business cards, banner, and signage. (4) All 9 marketing services — visitor ID, email, SMS, AI phone, Facebook ads, Google ads, geo-fencing, geo-grid tracking, and CRM. Everything is designed, built, and launched by our team.

How long does it take to launch?

6-8 weeks from kickoff. Weeks 1-2 are brand strategy and identity design. Weeks 3-5 are website development and print production (these happen in parallel). Weeks 6-8 are marketing stack deployment and QA. You approve everything at each phase.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We offer a 3-payment plan tied to project phases: $5,000 at signing to start brand identity, $4,500 when website development begins, and $3,000 at marketing launch. Monthly marketing services ($2,497/mo) begin once all systems are live.

What if I already have a logo or website?

We customize. If your existing brand identity is strong, we refine instead of rebuilding and apply the savings toward upgraded print or web components. If your website needs a refresh, same approach. Brand System is flexible — we meet you where you are.

What happens after launch?

Your $2,497/mo covers all 9 marketing services — managed, optimized, and reported on monthly. You also get priority support for website updates. After the initial 6-month marketing commitment, you can continue month-to-month, adjust services, or step down to a smaller bundle.

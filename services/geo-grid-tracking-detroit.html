Overview

When you search 'branding agency near me' from your office, you might rank #1. But when someone searches the same thing from 3 miles away, you might not even show up. Google Maps rankings change dramatically based on the searcher's exact location — and most businesses have no idea where they actually rank.

Geo-grid tracking solves this by scanning your rankings from 49 different points across your service area (a 7×7 grid). The result is a visual heat map showing exactly where you rank well (green), where you're average (yellow), and where you're invisible (red). With this data, we build a targeted strategy to improve your rankings in the specific areas where you're weakest.

How the Grid Works

We overlay a 7×7 grid across your target service area. At each of the 49 intersection points, we run your target keywords through Google Maps and record your ranking. This gets repeated for each keyword you're tracking (up to 10). The result: 490 data points showing your exact visibility across your market. No guessing, no assumptions — just data.

Frequently Asked Questions

GEO-GRID TRACKING FAQ

Why do Google Maps rankings change by location?

Google uses proximity as a major ranking factor. The closer a searcher is to your business, the more likely you are to rank well. But other factors matter too — reviews, GBP completeness, citations, website SEO, and category relevance. Geo-grid tracking reveals exactly where proximity is helping or hurting you.

How often are scans performed?

We run full grid scans monthly, with weekly spot-checks on your most important keywords and locations. You receive a comprehensive monthly report with heat maps, trend charts, and specific recommendations.

Can I track my competitors?

Yes. Every scan shows not just your ranking but your top competitors' rankings at every grid point. You'll see exactly where each competitor outranks you and where you have the advantage.

Does this include Google Business Profile optimization?

The tracking plan includes recommendations for GBP optimization. If you need hands-on GBP management (posting, review responses, photo uploads, Q&A management), we offer that as an add-on service.

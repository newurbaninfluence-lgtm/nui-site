Services & Details

What Makes a Great Small Business Website

A great website does three things: it loads fast (under 3 seconds or you lose 53% of visitors), it looks professional on every device, and it makes it dead simple for someone to take the next step — whether that's booking a consultation, placing an order, or calling your business.

We build every site with these principles. No bloated page builders that take 8 seconds to load. No desktop-only designs that look broken on phones. No mystery navigation where customers can't figure out how to contact you.

  • Mobile-first design (not just "mobile-friendly")
  • Page load speed under 3 seconds
  • Clear calls-to-action on every page
  • SEO-optimized structure and content
  • ADA accessibility compliance
  • SSL security certificate
  • Google Analytics and Search Console setup
  • Integration with your existing tools

Websites Built for Detroit Businesses

We've built websites for Detroit restaurants that need online ordering and reservations. For service businesses that need booking systems and lead capture forms. For product brands that need e-commerce stores with inventory management. For coaches and consultants that need sales funnels and webinar registration.

Every industry has different needs, and we customize each site to match. A restaurant website is fundamentally different from a law firm website — we don't treat them the same.

We Don't Just Build — We Optimize

Every website includes SEO optimization so Google can find you. We set up your Google Business Profile link, schema markup for local search, meta tags for every page, and fast-loading images. Most "web designers" skip this step entirely and hand you a beautiful site that nobody can find. We make sure your investment actually generates traffic and leads.

Your Website, Your Control

We build on platforms you can manage yourself — no calling us to change a phone number or update a photo. Every site comes with a CMS (content management system) and a training session so you or your team can make basic updates. For businesses that want hands-off management, we offer monthly maintenance plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

WEB DESIGN FAQ

How much does a website cost in Detroit?

At New Urban Influence, website design starts at $3,500 for a single-page site, $5,500 for a full multi-page business website, and $7,500 for an e-commerce store. All packages include mobile-responsive design, SEO optimization, and a training session. Payment plans available.

How long does it take to build a website?

A single-page website takes 2-3 weeks. Multi-page business websites take 4-6 weeks. E-commerce stores take 6-8 weeks. Timeline depends on content readiness — we move fastest when you have your copy, photos, and brand assets ready.

Do you build on WordPress, Shopify, or custom code?

We match the platform to your needs. WordPress for content-heavy sites and blogs. Shopify for e-commerce. Custom-coded for maximum performance and unique requirements. We recommend the best option during your free consultation.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Every website we build is mobile-first — meaning we design for phones first, then scale up to tablets and desktops. Since 70%+ of web traffic is mobile, this ensures the best experience for the majority of your visitors.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes — we offer monthly maintenance plans starting at $150/month that include security updates, backups, minor content changes, uptime monitoring, and performance optimization. Or you can manage it yourself using the CMS we set up.

Will my website show up on Google?

Every site includes SEO optimization — proper page titles, meta descriptions, schema markup, image optimization, fast load times, and Google Search Console setup. We also connect your site to your Google Business Profile for local search visibility.

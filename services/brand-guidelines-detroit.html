Overview

You invested in a professional brand identity. Now you need to protect that investment. Brand guidelines are the rulebook that ensures your brand looks consistent whether it's on a business card, a billboard, or a social media post. Without guidelines, your brand slowly drifts off-course as different people interpret your identity differently. With guidelines, every touchpoint reinforces the same professional image.

Services & Details

What's Included in Brand Guidelines

A comprehensive brand guidelines document covers every aspect of your visual and verbal identity. It's the single reference point that anyone — employees, freelancers, printers, developers — can use to represent your brand correctly.

  • Logo usage rules and clear space requirements
  • Incorrect logo usage examples (what NOT to do)
  • Primary and secondary color palette with hex, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone codes
  • Typography hierarchy with font pairing rules
  • Photography and imagery style direction
  • Icon and graphic element standards
  • Social media usage guidelines
  • Brand voice and messaging guidelines
  • Print and digital specifications
  • Co-branding and partnership rules

Why Brand Guidelines Matter

Consistent branding increases revenue by up to 23% according to Forbes. When your brand looks the same everywhere, customers develop trust and recognition faster. Brand guidelines prevent the gradual erosion of your identity that happens when different team members, freelancers, and vendors make their own interpretations of your brand. They're an investment in long-term brand equity.

Digital-First Brand Guidelines

We create brand guidelines as interactive digital documents — not just static PDFs that collect dust. Your guidelines live online, are easy to share, and include downloadable assets. When you hire a new designer or marketing agency, just send them the link. Everything they need is in one place.

Frequently Asked Questions

STYLE GUIDES FAQ

Are brand guidelines included with NUI branding packages?

Yes — brand guidelines are included with every NUI branding package. Our Brand Kit ($1,500) includes essential logo usage and color guidelines. The Service and Product Brand Identity packages ($4,500-$5,500) include comprehensive multi-page brand books with detailed usage rules.

Can you create brand guidelines for an existing brand?

Yes — if you already have a brand identity but lack formal guidelines, we can document and formalize your brand standards. This standalone service starts at $1,000 and includes a full audit of your existing brand assets.

What format are the brand guidelines delivered in?

We deliver brand guidelines as both an interactive digital document (shareable link) and a downloadable PDF. The digital version includes direct download links for all brand assets.

How often should brand guidelines be updated?

Brand guidelines should be reviewed annually and updated whenever you add new brand elements, enter new markets, or evolve your visual identity. We offer update packages for existing clients.

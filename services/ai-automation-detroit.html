Questions

What People Actually Ask.

Do I have to manage any of this myself?

No. We build it, configure it, train it, and maintain it. You approve the setup, review the weekly reports, and use the output. We handle everything technical.

How quickly can these be set up?

Most services go live within 7–14 days of onboarding. The AI Phone Assistant and Custom AI Assistant take 2–3 weeks because they require deep business training. Pixel and tracking services go live in 48–72 hours.

Does the AI actually learn my business or is it generic?

It's trained on your business. We start with a discovery session — your services, your pricing, your FAQs, your voice, your scripts. The AI is then trained on all of that. Over time it learns from real conversations and gets sharper. It sounds like you, not like every other business using the same tool.

What's the setup fee for?

The setup fee covers the build, configuration, integration, and training. These are not plug-and-play tools — each one is custom-built for your business. The monthly fee covers maintenance, optimization, and ongoing support.

Do I need a Digital HQ first?

For most AI services, yes — your website is where the chatbot lives, where pixels are installed, where leads land. The AI Phone Assistant can be deployed without a full HQ. We'll tell you exactly what's needed in the strategy call.

How Sales Funnels Work

A sales funnel is the path someone takes from discovering your business to becoming a paying customer. Instead of hoping people find your website and call you, a funnel actively captures interest and guides people toward a purchase decision.

Here's a simple example: Someone sees your Facebook ad → clicks to a landing page → downloads a free guide (now you have their email) → receives 5 emails over 10 days that demonstrate your expertise → gets an offer to book a consultation → becomes a client. That entire sequence runs automatically once it's built.

Frequently Asked Questions

AUTOMATION FAQ

How much does marketing automation cost?

Marketing automation projects at NUI start at $1,500 for a basic funnel (landing page + email sequence). Complete lead generation systems with multiple funnels, CRM setup, and ad integration range from $3,000-7,500. Monthly management is available for ongoing optimization.

What kind of businesses need marketing automation?

Any business that depends on leads — coaches, consultants, agencies, professional services, e-commerce brands, real estate agents, healthcare practices, and more. If you need a steady flow of new customers and currently rely on referrals or manual outreach, automation will change your business.

How quickly will I see results from a sales funnel?

Most funnels start generating leads within the first week of launching, especially when paired with targeted ads. The full nurture cycle (from lead to customer) depends on your sales cycle — typically 2-6 weeks for service businesses and faster for product businesses.

Do I need to write the emails myself?

No — we handle all the copywriting. We interview you about your business, expertise, and customer pain points, then write every email in your brand voice. You review and approve before anything goes live.

What's the difference between a landing page and a website?

A website is your online home with multiple pages. A landing page is a single focused page with one goal — capture a lead, sell a product, or register for an event. Landing pages convert 2-5x better than general website pages because they eliminate distractions and focus on one action.

Can you run my Facebook and Instagram ads too?

Yes — we offer ad management as part of our automation packages or as a standalone service. We create the ad creative, set up targeting, and connect everything to your funnel for seamless lead capture and follow-up.

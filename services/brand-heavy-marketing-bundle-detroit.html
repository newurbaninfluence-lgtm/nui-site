BRAND HEAVY BUNDLE FAQ

Can I start with a smaller bundle and upgrade later?

Yes. Many clients start with Brand Ready ($497/mo) or Brand Loaded ($1,497/mo) and upgrade to Brand Heavy as they grow. When you upgrade, we credit your previous setup fees toward the Brand Heavy setup cost.

Is there a contract?

Brand Heavy runs on a 6-month initial commitment, then month-to-month. This gives us enough time to fully deploy all 9 services, optimize performance, and demonstrate ROI. After 6 months, you can continue, adjust, or cancel.

Do I get a dedicated strategist?

Yes. Brand Heavy clients get a dedicated marketing strategist who manages all 9 services, conducts quarterly strategy reviews, and proactively identifies optimization opportunities. This level of support is exclusive to the Brand Heavy tier.

What results should I expect?

Within the first 30 days, you'll have all systems live and generating data. By month 3, you'll see clear patterns in lead generation, ad performance, and local rankings. By month 6, most Brand Heavy clients see a 3-5x return on their marketing investment. Results vary by industry and market, but total market coverage gives you every possible advantage.

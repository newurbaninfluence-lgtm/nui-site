Questions About
The Publicist.

What is The Publicist service?

The Publicist writes and publishes a professionally crafted profile of your business in NUI Magazine — a third-party credibility signal that builds trust before prospects ever call you. Features start at $1,500 and include the written profile, digital publication, permanent URL, and a "Featured In" badge for your website.

Is NUI Magazine a real publication?

Yes. NUI Magazine is a digital publication produced by New Urban Influence covering Detroit business, culture, and entrepreneurship. Features are professionally written editorial profiles, permanently accessible at newurbaninfluence.com/magazine, and indexed by Google.

Who benefits most from a press feature?

The Publicist is most powerful for businesses where trust drives the buying decision — roofing and renovation contractors (homeowners spending $10,000–$50,000 need to trust you), authors and speakers (a press feature validates your expertise), photographers, cannabis dispensaries, medical practices, attorneys, and consultants competing against more established names.

How long does it take to get featured?

Standard Feature turnaround is approximately 2 weeks from your intake call to publication. The Bundle with photography takes 3–4 weeks. You review and approve the profile before it publishes — you always have final say.

Questions

Common Questions.

Is NUI Magazine a real publication?

Yes. NUI Magazine is a digital publication produced by New Urban Influence covering Detroit business, culture, and entrepreneurship. Features are professionally written editorial profiles — permanently accessible and indexed by Google.

Who benefits most from a press feature?

Businesses where trust drives the buying decision — roofing, renovation, legal, medical, consulting, photography, cannabis, and authors. For high-ticket services where prospects are comparing options, a press feature is the easiest credibility upgrade available.

Do I get to review the feature before it publishes?

Yes. You approve the profile before it goes live. We revise until you're satisfied. You always have final say on what's published.

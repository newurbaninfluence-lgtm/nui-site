Questions

Common Questions.

Is NUI Magazine a real publication?

Yes. NUI Magazine is a digital publication produced by New Urban Influence — permanently accessible online, indexed by Google, and read by Detroit's business community. Your feature is an editorial profile, not an ad.

Do I get to approve the feature before it publishes?

Yes, always. We write the profile, share it with you for review, and revise until you're completely satisfied. Nothing publishes without your sign-off. You'll also be credited as a source, not just a subject.

How do I use the feature once it's published?

Share the link in proposal emails, your email signature, your website's about page, your LinkedIn profile, and anywhere a credibility signal would move the needle. The "As Featured in NUI Magazine" badge goes on your site and printed materials. Use it everywhere you need to build trust fast.

How long does it take?

The Feature package delivers in approximately 2 weeks from your intake call to publication. The Bundle with photography takes 3–4 weeks. Rush turnarounds available on request.

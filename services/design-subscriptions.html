Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as "one design"?

One unique piece — a social post, flyer, menu, banner ad, etc. Multi-page booklets use your book/catalog slot instead.

What happens to unused designs?

Credits reset each billing cycle and don't roll over. Plan your content calendar in advance to maximize your allocation.

Can I upgrade mid-month?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. The price difference is prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

Is hosting really included?

Yes — website or landing page hosting is included in all tiers for the duration of your active subscription.

What if I need something outside my plan?

Brand identity packages, websites, and other services are available separately. Subscribers get 10% off all one-time projects.

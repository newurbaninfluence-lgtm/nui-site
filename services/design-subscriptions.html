Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as "one design"?

One design = one unique piece of creative. A social media post, a flyer, a menu, a banner ad — each counts as one. Multi-page documents like booklets use your book/catalog slot instead.

What happens to unused designs?

Unused credits reset each billing cycle. They do not roll over. Use them or lose them — we recommend planning your content calendar in advance.

Can I upgrade mid-month?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. The price difference is prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

What if I need something outside my plan?

Brand identity packages, websites, and other services are available as separate projects. Subscription clients receive 10% off all one-time projects.

Questions About The
Digital Street Team.

What does the Digital Street Team include?

Four positions: The Content Crew (social media content, photos, scheduling), The Digital Promoter (SMS campaigns, push notifications, retargeting), The Block Captain (geofencing — targeting competitor locations and events), and The Neighborhood Captain (geo-gridding — owning Google Maps rankings). Hire one or the full team.

How much does social media management cost in Detroit?

NUI's Content Crew starts at $497/mo for Posted Up (3 posts/week, one platform). Loaded is $1,497/mo (daily content, two platforms, community management). Heavy is $2,997/mo (full production, all platforms, paid ad management included).

What is geofencing and how does it work for Detroit businesses?

Geofencing draws a virtual boundary around any location — a competitor's parking lot, a stadium on game night, a neighborhood. When someone's phone enters that zone, your ad appears on their device and follows them for up to 30 days. NUI's Block Captain handles geofencing, available with HQ Command. Importantly, geofencing uses programmatic display advertising — not Google or Facebook — so it works even for businesses with platform content restrictions.

Can bars and nightclubs run geofencing ads?

Yes. Geofencing uses programmatic display advertising which doesn't carry the same content restrictions as Facebook Ads or Google Ads. For bars, clubs, strip clubs, and cannabis dispensaries that can't run traditional social media ads, geofencing is one of the most effective paid visibility strategies available.

What is geo-gridding?

Geo-gridding is a systematic strategy for dominating Google Maps rankings across every zip code in your service territory. The Neighborhood Captain maps out your entire area, then works your Google Business Profile to rank in each square. When someone in Southfield, Dearborn, Royal Oak, or anywhere in your territory searches for what you do, your business shows up first — not because you paid for an ad, but because your territory was worked consistently.

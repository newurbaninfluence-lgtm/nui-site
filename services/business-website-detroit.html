Questions About
Digital HQ.

How much does a business website cost in Detroit?

Digital HQ Lite starts at $3,500 for a 3-page foundation site with GMB setup. HQ Standard is $5,500 and includes unlimited pages, full SEO, citations, and Meta Pixel. HQ Command at $8,500+ adds Google Ads, automation, and geo-grid mapping. All levels available with 0% financing from $125/month through Afterpay or Klarna.

What is the difference between a Digital HQ and a regular website?

A regular website is a digital brochure. A Digital HQ is the infrastructure your business runs on — website, local SEO foundation, Google Business optimization, citation network, lead capture system, and the technical setup that unlocks your Digital Staff team. Your nephew can build a website. The Store Manager builds a business system.

Will my website show up on Google?

HQ Standard and HQ Command include full local SEO setup — Google Business Profile optimization, 50+ citation directory listings, on-page SEO, schema markup, and sitemap. Your site is built to rank from day one. HQ Lite includes basic GMB setup and foundational SEO.

How long does it take to build a Digital HQ?

HQ Lite delivers in approximately 2 weeks. HQ Standard takes 2–3 weeks. HQ Command takes 3–4 weeks. All timelines include design mockup approval, development, testing, SEO setup, and a live training walkthrough.

Can I upgrade my HQ level later?

Yes. Most clients start with HQ Lite to get established and upgrade to Standard or Command as their business grows. Upgrades are credited — you only pay the difference between levels, never the full price again.

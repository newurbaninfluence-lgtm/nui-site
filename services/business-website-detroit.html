Questions

Common Questions.

What's the difference between a Digital HQ and a regular website?

A regular website is a brochure. A Digital HQ is a business system — it captures leads, books appointments, collects payments, and feeds your CRM automatically. It also determines which AI staff positions you can hire to run the business alongside you.

Do I need a brand before building a Digital HQ?

Recommended. We can work with existing brand assets or include The Blueprint as part of a Grand Opening package. We'll tell you what we need after our strategy call.

How long does it take to build?

HQ Lite is typically 2–3 weeks. HQ Standard is 3–4 weeks. HQ Command is 4–6 weeks depending on scope and integrations.

What happens after it's built?

You get full ownership of your HQ. We offer monthly maintenance packages if you want us to keep running it — or we hand it off with documentation so your team can manage it. Your HQ, your asset.

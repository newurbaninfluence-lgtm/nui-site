Questions

Common Questions.

What's the difference between a Digital HQ and a regular website?

A regular website tells people about you. A Digital HQ captures leads, books appointments, collects payments, and feeds your CRM — automatically. It also determines which AI staff positions you can hire to run the operation alongside you.

Do I need The Blueprint first?

Recommended. A Digital HQ built on a weak brand is still a weak brand. We can build both together through the Grand Opening package, or sequence them — Blueprint first, then HQ.

How long does it take to build?

HQ Lite is 2–3 weeks. HQ Standard is 3–4 weeks. HQ Command is 4–6 weeks depending on integrations. Timeline starts after a completed discovery session.

Do I own it after it's built?

Completely. Your HQ is your asset — not ours. We offer monthly maintenance packages if you want ongoing support, or we hand it off with documentation so your team can manage it.

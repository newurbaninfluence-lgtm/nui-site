Questions About
Digital Staff.

What is the Digital Secretary and how does it work?

The Digital Secretary is NUI's AI receptionist that answers every call, qualifies every lead, and books appointments 24/7 — for $197/month. It replaces a traditional receptionist or answering service costing $2,800–$3,500/month plus benefits. It never misses a call, never takes a lunch break, and works while you're on a job.

What is the Lead Catcher?

The Lead Catcher identifies anonymous website visitors by name and email without them filling out a form. When someone visits your site, browses your services, and leaves without contacting you — the Lead Catcher identifies them and automatically sends a personalized follow-up based on exactly what they looked at. Requires HQ Standard.

What is the Digital Promoter?

The Digital Promoter handles your owned-audience outreach — SMS campaigns and web push notifications to customers who opted in — plus retargeting ads that follow website visitors who left without converting. Two channels, one position, working continuously.

What is the Neighborhood Captain?

The Neighborhood Captain covers geofencing and geo-gridding. Geofencing places your ad in front of anyone whose phone enters a location you choose — a competitor's lot, a stadium, a neighborhood. Geo-gridding systematically works your Google Maps ranking across every zip code in your service territory. Requires HQ Command.

Do I need a Digital HQ first?

The Digital Secretary can be added to any HQ level including Lite. The Lead Catcher and Digital Promoter require HQ Standard. The Neighborhood Captain requires HQ Command. If you don't have a Digital HQ yet, we build the right level first — then staff it.

Questions

Common Questions.

Do I need a Digital HQ to hire Digital Staff?

Yes. Digital Staff positions run out of your HQ — the website, CRM, and lead capture system are what they operate on. No HQ means no infrastructure for the staff to work from.

Does this replace a real receptionist?

For most small businesses, yes. The Digital Secretary answers calls, books appointments, and sends confirmations 24/7. For complex conversations that need a human, calls are routed to you directly.

What happens when a lead comes in overnight?

The Lead Catcher fires within 60 seconds — regardless of the time. They get an immediate response, their questions get answered, and an estimate gets requested. By morning, you have a qualified lead waiting.

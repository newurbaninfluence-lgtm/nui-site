Questions

Common Questions.

Do I need a Digital HQ first?

Yes. Digital Staff positions run from your HQ — the website, lead capture system, and CRM are the infrastructure the staff operates on. No HQ means no place for the staff to work from.

Does the Digital Secretary actually answer calls?

Yes. It handles inbound calls, collects information, answers common questions, and books appointments. For calls that need a human, it routes them to you or your team. You stay involved where it matters, and the AI handles the rest.

What happens to a lead that comes in at 2am?

The Lead Catcher responds within minutes — regardless of the time. By morning, you have a qualified lead that's been followed up with, answered, and potentially booked. You wake up to work, not chase.

Is this different from a chatbot on my website?

Significantly. Website chatbots capture on-site traffic only. Digital Staff works across all channels — phone, web, Google, SMS, and push — and integrates with your CRM so nothing is siloed.

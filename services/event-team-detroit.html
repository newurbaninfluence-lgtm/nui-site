Why The Push Opt-In Is The Key

Most opt-ins are passive — enter your email for 10% off. People give a fake email, unsubscribe the next day, or just ignore it. The Event Team creates active desire before the ask: they want the photo. The opt-in is the gate. They say yes because they want what's on the other side. And once they do — you own direct access to their phone, not rented access through someone else's platform.

Questions About
The Event Team.

What is The Event Team?

The Event Team is NUI's day-rate Digital Staff deployment for vendor shows, pop-up shops, and events. Four positions work together at your table: The Photographer (branded photos), The Sign-In Sheet (data collection), The Delivery Person (SMS photo + store link in 60 seconds), and The Door Person (push notification opt-in gate). Every person who stops at your booth becomes a verified, opted-in lead.

How much does The Event Team cost?

Half Day (up to 4 hours, 100 captures) is $497. Full Day (8 hours, unlimited captures, post-event lead report) is $897. Weekend (unlimited, automated follow-up fires Monday) is $1,497. Custom branded photo overlay is $150 add-on.

Is this built for All Things Detroit vendors?

Yes. NUI has a relationship with All Things Detroit and built their brand. The Event Team was designed specifically for the ATD vendor experience — foot traffic and great product, but no system to capture that interest and convert it into customers who come back. For $497 you leave with a verified database of everyone who stopped at your table.

What happens with the leads after the event?

Every lead automatically feeds into your Digital Staff system. The Street Announcer reaches them via SMS. The Lead Catcher follows up with visitors who browsed your store link. The Digital Promoter retargets them with ads. If you have a Digital HQ, leads go directly into your CRM with an event tag. The Event Team doesn't just work the event — it feeds your whole system for weeks after.

Questions

Common Questions.

Is this built for All Things Detroit vendors?

Yes. NUI built the All Things Detroit brand and the Event Team was designed specifically for the ATD vendor experience — foot traffic and great product, but no system to turn that interest into repeat customers. For $497 you leave with a verified database of everyone who stopped at your table.

What happens with the leads after the event?

Every lead feeds your Digital Staff system automatically. The Street Announcer reaches them via SMS. The Lead Catcher follows up with anyone who browsed your store link but didn't buy. The Digital Promoter retargets them with ads. The Event Team doesn't just work the event — it feeds your whole system for weeks after.

Do I need a Digital HQ for the Event Team?

No — the Event Team works standalone. You get the lead list regardless of your HQ level. If you have an HQ, leads sync directly to your CRM. If not, we deliver a clean spreadsheet post-event.

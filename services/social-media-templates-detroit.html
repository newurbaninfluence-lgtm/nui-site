Overview

You know you need to post on social media. You know consistency matters. But between running your business, serving customers, and actually living your life — who has time to design professional-looking posts every day? That's where custom social media templates change the game. Instead of starting from scratch every time, you get a branded toolkit that makes creating content as easy as swapping out text and photos.

Services & Details

What's in a Social Media Kit

Our social media kits are built in Canva so you can edit them yourself — no design software needed. Every template matches your brand identity with your exact colors, fonts, and visual style. Just swap the text and photos, and you have a professional post ready in minutes.

  • 15-30 branded Instagram/Facebook post templates
  • Story templates (promo, quote, behind-the-scenes)
  • Reel/TikTok cover templates
  • LinkedIn post and article templates
  • Highlight cover icons
  • Profile picture and banner templates
  • Content calendar template
  • Hashtag strategy guide

Why Templates Beat Hiring a Social Media Manager

A social media manager costs $1,500-5,000/month. Our template kit is a one-time investment starting at $500 that lets you or your team create professional content indefinitely. You maintain control of your brand voice, post when you want, and never wait for approvals. For small businesses and startups, templates are the smart play.

Platforms We Design For

Instagram is king for visual brands — we design feed posts, Stories, Reels covers, and carousel templates. Facebook gets adapted versions optimized for that platform's dimensions. LinkedIn templates are designed for professional audiences with a different visual approach. TikTok covers and Pinterest pins round out the kit for businesses on those platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

DETROIT FAQ

How much do custom social media templates cost?

Social media template kits at NUI start at $500 for a basic set of 15 templates. Our Brand Kit package ($1,500) includes social media templates as part of a complete brand identity. For ongoing social media management, we offer monthly content creation packages.

What software do I need to edit the templates?

All templates are built in Canva (free version works). No Adobe software or design experience needed. We deliver via Canva link so you can duplicate and edit instantly from any device.

Can I share the templates with my team?

Yes — Canva templates can be shared with your entire team via link. Everyone gets access to the same branded templates, ensuring consistent posting no matter who creates the content.

Do you offer monthly social media management?

Yes — for businesses that want hands-off social media, we offer monthly content creation and management packages. Contact us for custom pricing based on posting frequency and platforms.

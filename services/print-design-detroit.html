Overview

Your business doesn't just live online. From trade shows and storefronts to events and direct mail campaigns, print graphics are how you show up in the real world. In Detroit, where word-of-mouth and local presence drive business, a well-designed banner, business card, or vehicle magnet can generate more leads than a dozen social media posts. We handle everything — design, printing, and delivery across 30+ product types. You approve the proof, we send it to our wholesale print partner for 24-hour production, and deliver the finished product to your door. No dealing with printers, no file format headaches, no surprises.

What We Design

Banners & Large Format

Whether it's a retractable banner for a trade show, a hanging banner for your storefront, or a step-and-repeat backdrop for your event, we design banners that pop. Every banner is designed at full resolution with proper bleed so your print comes out crisp and professional every time.

  • Retractable / pull-up banners
  • Vinyl banners (indoor and outdoor)
  • Trade show booth graphics
  • Step-and-repeat event backdrops
  • Storefront window banners
  • Hanging banners and ceiling graphics

Posters & Wall Graphics

From promotional posters for events to wall art for your office or retail space, we design posters that communicate your message clearly and look stunning at any size. Perfect for restaurants, barbershops, salons, retail stores, and event promotions.

  • Event and promotional posters
  • Menu boards and restaurant posters
  • Office wall graphics and motivational signage
  • Retail promotional displays
  • Concert and event flyers (large format)

Yard Signs & Outdoor Signage

Yard signs are one of the most cost-effective forms of local advertising in Detroit. We design yard signs for real estate agents, political campaigns, business promotions, grand openings, church events, and more. Double-sided designs, bold colors, and layouts that are readable from the street.

  • Real estate yard signs
  • Political and campaign signs
  • Business promotion and sale signs
  • Grand opening and event signs
  • Directional and wayfinding signs
  • A-frame / sandwich board designs

Event Backgrounds & Backdrops

First impressions at events start with your backdrop. We design photo-worthy event backgrounds for galas, pop-up shops, product launches, birthday parties, corporate events, and brand activations. Your brand front and center in every photo that gets shared on social media.

  • Step-and-repeat backdrops
  • Photo booth backgrounds
  • Stage and presentation backdrops
  • Pop-up shop displays
  • Branded tent and canopy graphics

Vinyl Decals & Window Graphics

Vinyl decals turn your storefront, windows, walls, and vehicles into 24/7 advertising. We design custom vinyl decals, window graphics, wall decals, and floor graphics that are precision-cut and ready for any surface. From small logo decals to full storefront window wraps.

  • Storefront window graphics & perforated vinyl
  • One-way vision window film
  • Static cling window graphics
  • Logo and business name decals
  • Wall decals for offices and retail
  • Floor graphics and directional decals
  • Die-cut stickers and labels

Vehicle Magnets

Turn your car, truck, or van into a mobile billboard. Vehicle magnets are one of the most cost-effective advertising tools in Detroit — removable, reusable, and visible everywhere you drive. We design bold, eye-catching magnets that make your business impossible to miss on the road.

  • Car door magnets (12x24 and 18x24)
  • Truck and van magnets
  • Fleet vehicle magnet sets
  • UV-laminated for weather resistance
  • Removable — no damage to vehicle

Business Cards & Postcards

First impressions start with your business card. We design and print premium 16pt business cards and postcards that feel substantial and look professional. From minimalist modern designs to bold full-color layouts, every card is designed to make people keep it instead of toss it.

  • 16pt premium cardstock business cards
  • UV gloss or matte coating options
  • 4x6 and 6x9 postcards and mailers
  • EDDM and direct mail ready
  • Quantities from 250 to 10,000+

Acrylic, Dibond & Premium Signage

When you need signage that makes a statement, premium substrates deliver. Acrylic signs give your lobby or retail space a modern, high-end look. Dibond (aluminum composite) panels are the gold standard for exterior signage — weatherproof, rigid, and built to last 10+ years. Foam core is perfect for events, presentations, and indoor displays.

  • Clear acrylic signs with UV direct print
  • Dibond / aluminum composite exterior signs
  • Foam core mounted prints and displays
  • Lobby signs and wayfinding
  • Real estate and event signage
  • Custom sizes and shapes available

Frequently Asked Questions

DETROIT PRINT FAQ

How much does banner and poster design cost in Detroit?

We offer turnkey design + print packages. Vinyl banners start at $175, yard signs from $35 each (minimum 10), retractable banners from $275 including hardware, business cards from $195 for 250, vehicle magnets from $195 per pair, acrylic signs from $275, and Dibond panels from $325. All prices include professional design, printing, and finishing. Volume discounts available.

Do you print the banners and signs too?

Yes! Every package includes both design and professional printing. We handle everything — you approve the design, we send it to print, and deliver the finished product to you. Our wholesale print partner offers 24-hour production with overnight shipping available. You get turnkey convenience with agency-quality design and commercial-grade printing.

What file formats do I get for print design?

Every print design project includes high-resolution PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), and PNG files. All files are CMYK color mode with proper bleed marks and trim lines. For vinyl decals and cut graphics, we provide vector files with cut lines ready for any vinyl cutter or sign shop.

Can you design yard signs for my campaign or event?

Yes — we design yard signs for political campaigns, real estate, business promotions, events, grand openings, and more. Standard sizes include 18x24 and 24x36 inches. We provide print-ready files and can help you order from local Detroit sign printers for fast turnaround.

How long does print design take?

Most print design projects take 1-2 weeks including revisions. Simple items like yard signs or business cards can be ready in 3-5 business days. Larger projects like trade show booth sets or multi-piece event packages take 2-3 weeks. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive projects.

Do you design and print business cards?

Yes — we design and print premium 16pt business cards starting at $195 for 250 cards. Every order includes custom design (front and back), full color printing, and your choice of UV gloss or matte coating. We also offer postcards starting at $225 for 250 cards, perfect for direct mail campaigns and EDDM mailings.

Can you make vehicle magnets for my business?

Absolutely — vehicle magnets are one of our most popular products. A pair of 12x24" magnets starts at $195 and a pair of 18x24" magnets starts at $275, both including custom design. They're UV-laminated for weather resistance, removable with no damage to your vehicle, and turn your car or truck into a mobile billboard everywhere you go.

What premium sign materials do you offer?

We offer three premium substrates: acrylic signs starting at $275 (clear UV-printed, perfect for lobbies and modern interiors), Dibond aluminum composite panels starting at $325 (weatherproof exterior signage rated for 10+ years), and foam core boards starting at $195 (lightweight, ideal for events, presentations, and real estate). All include custom design and professional printing.

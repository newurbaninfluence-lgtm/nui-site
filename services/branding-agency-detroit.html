What Makes a Great Brand Identity

A brand identity isn't just a logo. It's the complete system that determines how your business looks, feels, and communicates across every touchpoint. When a customer sees your business card, visits your website, scrolls past your Instagram post, and walks into your location — the experience should be seamless and intentional.

  • Brand strategy and positioning
  • Logo design (primary, secondary, icon)
  • Color system with accessibility checks
  • Typography hierarchy
  • Photography and visual direction
  • Social media templates and kit
  • Business cards and stationery
  • Brand guidelines document
  • Presentation templates
  • Email signatures

Frequently Asked Questions

AGENCY FAQ

What does a branding agency do?

A branding agency creates the visual and strategic identity for your business. This includes your logo, colors, typography, imagery style, brand voice, and all the marketing materials that bring these elements together. At NUI, we also include brand strategy — defining your positioning, target audience, and competitive advantages.

How much does branding cost for a small business?

At New Urban Influence, small business branding packages start at $1,500 for a Brand Kit and go up to $5,500 for a complete Product Brand Identity. All packages include strategy, logo design, color system, typography, and brand guidelines. Payment plans are available on every package.

How do I choose the right branding agency in Detroit?

Look for an agency that includes brand strategy (not just design), shows real results in their portfolio, has transparent pricing, and specializes in businesses your size. Avoid agencies that jump straight to design without understanding your business — that's decoration, not branding.

What's the difference between branding and logo design?

Logo design is one element of branding. A full brand identity includes your logo, color palette, typography, imagery direction, brand voice, marketing templates, and guidelines for consistent use. Think of the logo as the face — branding is the entire personality.

Do you work with businesses outside Detroit?

Yes — while we're based in Detroit and serve the Metro Detroit area, we work with clients nationwide. Our process is fully remote-capable with video strategy sessions, digital proof reviews through our client portal, and file delivery.

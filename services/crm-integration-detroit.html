Overview

When your marketing tools don't talk to each other, leads fall through the cracks. Someone fills out a form and it sits in your inbox. A visitor gets identified but nobody follows up. A phone call comes in and the notes live on a sticky note. You're paying for marketing tools but losing leads because there's no central system connecting them.

CRM integration brings everything into one dashboard. Every lead, every interaction, every touchpoint — visible in one place. When a visitor gets identified by Silent Visitor ID, they automatically appear in your CRM. When they open an email, it's logged. When they call and the AI assistant answers, the transcript is attached. Your sales pipeline becomes a machine instead of a mess.

Why One Dashboard Matters

Without a CRM, your marketing data lives in silos. Email stats are in Mailchimp. Visitor data is in RB2B. Ad performance is in Google. Phone calls are in your call log. It takes 30 minutes just to figure out what happened with a single lead. With CRM integration, you open one dashboard and see the complete picture: who this person is, where they came from, what they've seen, and what action to take next.

Frequently Asked Questions

CRM INTEGRATION FAQ

Which CRM platforms do you support?

We primarily work with HubSpot (free and paid tiers), GoHighLevel, and Salesforce. If you already have a CRM, we'll integrate with it. If you need one, we'll recommend the best option for your business size and budget.

Is there a monthly fee after setup?

The CRM integration is a $1,000 one-time setup fee. There are no ongoing management fees from NUI. However, your CRM platform itself may have monthly costs (HubSpot free tier is available, GoHighLevel starts at $97/mo, Salesforce starts at $25/user/mo).

Can this integrate with non-NUI tools?

Yes. We can connect your CRM to most popular business tools using native integrations or Zapier — including your website forms, booking systems, accounting software, and more.

How long does setup take?

Typically 1-2 weeks depending on how many services and tools need to be connected. We handle all the technical configuration and provide a training session so you and your team are comfortable using the system.

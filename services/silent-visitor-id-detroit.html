Right now, 97% of the people who visit your website leave without doing anything. No form fill, no phone call, no email. They looked at your services, maybe checked your pricing, and disappeared. You'll never know they were there — unless you have Silent Visitor ID.

What You Get

A complete visitor identification system installed on your website, configured and optimized for your business.

  • RB2B pixel installation and configuration
  • Real-time lead dashboard with visitor profiles
  • Full contact info: name, email, company, job title, LinkedIn
  • Page view history — see exactly what each visitor looked at
  • Weekly summary reports delivered to your inbox

How It Works

We install a small tracking pixel on your website (takes 5 minutes). When someone visits, the system matches their device data against a database of 200M+ professionals. If there's a match, their full profile appears in your dashboard within seconds. You see their name, email, company, what pages they viewed, how long they stayed, and whether they've visited before. No forms required. No popups. Completely invisible to the visitor.

Frequently Asked Questions

SILENT VISITOR ID FAQ

Is Silent Visitor ID legal?

Yes. Silent Visitor ID uses publicly available business data and complies with all US privacy regulations. It identifies business visitors using the same data available through LinkedIn and business directories. No personal browsing data is collected — only professional identity matching.

How many visitors can it identify?

Typically 15-30% of your total website traffic, depending on your audience. B2B traffic tends to have higher identification rates (20-30%) because more business professionals are in the database. B2C rates are lower but still valuable.

Do visitors know they're being identified?

No. The identification happens server-side using data matching. There are no popups, cookies banners, or visible indicators. The experience for your website visitor is completely unchanged.

Can I integrate this with my CRM?

Yes. We can push identified visitors directly into HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, or any CRM with an API. You can also set up automated email sequences that trigger when a high-value visitor is identified.

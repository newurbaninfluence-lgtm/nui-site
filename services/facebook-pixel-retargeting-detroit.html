How Retargeting Works

When someone visits your website, Meta Pixel drops a cookie on their browser. Now Facebook and Instagram know this person visited your site. You can then create ads that ONLY show to people who've already visited — meaning you're advertising to people who already know your brand and showed interest. This is why retargeting ads convert 3-5x better than cold ads: you're reaching warm leads, not strangers.

Frequently Asked Questions

FACEBOOK PIXEL & RETARGETING FAQ

Do I need a Facebook business page?

Yes, you need a Facebook Business Page and a Meta Business Suite account. If you don't have these, we'll set them up as part of the onboarding process at no extra charge.

How much should I spend on ads?

We recommend a minimum ad budget of $300-500/mo for retargeting and $500-1,000/mo if you want to add prospecting campaigns. The management fee ($199/mo) is separate from your ad spend, which goes directly to Meta.

Will this work for my local business?

Absolutely. We geo-target your ads to your service area, so you're only paying to reach people in your market. Whether you serve all of Metro Detroit or just a 5-mile radius, we dial in the targeting.

How quickly will I see results?

Retargeting campaigns typically show results within the first 1-2 weeks. It takes about 30 days to fully optimize audiences and creative. We provide monthly reports showing impressions, clicks, conversions, and cost per result.

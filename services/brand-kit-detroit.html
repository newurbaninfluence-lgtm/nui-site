The Problem

A Weak Brand Is
Costing You Jobs Every Day.

People decide if they trust you in seconds — before they read a word, before they call, before they step inside. That decision is made based on how you look. A weak brand doesn't just miss the sale. It actively sends customers to your competitor.

You look like everyone else

Generic logo, random colors, no visual system — customers can't tell you apart from the five other businesses that do the same thing.

You can't charge what you're worth

Premium pricing requires a premium brand. A Canva logo keeps your price ceiling low. Your brand signals what you're worth before you say a number.

Inconsistency erodes trust

Your website looks different from your card, which looks different from your Instagram. Every inconsistency is a signal that you're not running a real business.

No strategy behind the design

A logo is not a brand. A brand is a system — designed for your specific customer, market position, and growth goals. Most businesses never get this built correctly.

Questions

Common Questions.

How do I know which package I need?

Brand Kit = you're launching or refreshing and need a solid foundation. Service Brand = you're selling your expertise and need to look premium on proposals, at events, and in person. Product Brand = you're selling physical goods and need everything from label to shelf to social to work together. When in doubt, book the strategy call — we'll tell you straight.

How long does it take?

Brand Kit: 7–10 business days. Service Brand: 10–21 days. Product Brand: 14–28 days. Rush delivery available. Timeline starts after your discovery questionnaire is complete.

How many logo concepts will I see?

Three distinct concepts, each in a different creative direction. You choose one and we refine it until it's exactly right — unlimited revisions from that point forward.

I already have a logo. Can you work with it?

Maybe. We'll audit what you have honestly and tell you if it's worth refining or if starting fresh is the smarter call. No upsell — just a straight answer after seeing what you've got.

Do I need this before getting a Digital HQ?

Recommended. A Digital HQ built on a weak brand is still a weak brand. We can do both together through the Grand Opening package, or sequence them — brand first, then build the HQ on top of it.

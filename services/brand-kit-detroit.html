The Problem

A Weak Brand Costs You
Jobs Every Single Day.

People decide if they trust you in seconds — before they read a word, before they call, before they walk in. That snap judgment is based on how you look. A weak brand doesn't just miss the sale. It sends customers straight to your competitor.

You look like everyone else

Generic logo, random colors, no visual system — customers can't tell you apart from five other businesses doing exactly what you do.

You can't charge what you're worth

Premium pricing requires a premium brand. A Canva logo sets your price ceiling before you ever open your mouth.

Inconsistency kills trust

Website looks different from your card, your card looks different from your Instagram. Every mismatch signals you're not serious.

No strategy behind the design

A logo is not a brand. A brand is a system built for your specific customer, your market, and your growth goals. Most businesses never get this right.

How do I know which package I need?

Brand Kit = launching or refreshing on a focused budget. Service Brand = you sell your expertise and need to look premium on proposals and in person. Product Brand = you sell physical goods and need everything from label to shelf to social to work together. Not sure? Book the call — we'll tell you straight.

How long does it take?

Brand Kit: 7–10 business days. Service Brand: 10–21 days. Product Brand: 14–28 days. Rush delivery available. Timeline starts after your discovery questionnaire is complete.

How many logo concepts will I see?

Three distinct concepts exploring different creative directions. You choose one and we refine it until it's exactly right — unlimited revisions from that point forward.

I already have a logo. Can you work with it?

Maybe. We'll audit what you have honestly and tell you if it's worth refining or if starting fresh is the smarter call. No upsell — just a straight answer.

Do I need this before getting a Digital HQ?

Recommended. A Digital HQ built on a weak brand is still a weak brand. We can do both together through our Grand Opening package, or sequence them — brand first, then the HQ built on top.

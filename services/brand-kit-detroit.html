Questions

Common Questions.

How long does a brand build take?

Foundation packages deliver in 7–10 business days. Full Build is 10–21 days. Grand Opening is 3–4 weeks. Rush delivery is available.

How many logo concepts will I see?

We present 3 distinct concepts, each exploring a different creative direction. You choose one and we refine it until it's right — unlimited revisions included.

Do I need The Blueprint before getting a Digital HQ?

Recommended but not required. If you already have strong brand assets, we can build from those. If not, The Blueprint is the right first step — the HQ is built on top of your brand.

What if I already have a logo?

We can audit your existing brand and either refine what you have or start fresh. We'll tell you honestly which path makes more sense after seeing your current assets.

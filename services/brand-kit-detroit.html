The Problem

Your Brand Is
Working Against You.

A weak brand doesn't just look bad — it costs you jobs. Customers are making snap judgments about your business before they call, before they walk in, before they read a single word you've written.

You look like everyone else

Generic logos, random color choices, inconsistent fonts across your website, social, and cards — all of it signals "I'm not serious enough to invest in my appearance."

Customers pick the competitor

When two businesses solve the same problem, the one that looks more credible wins — even if their work is worse. Perception is the first filter.

You can't charge what you're worth

A Canva logo and a free website template keeps your price ceiling low. People pay premium prices for brands that feel premium.

Social media makes it worse

Every inconsistent post, every off-brand graphic, every mismatched font erodes trust one impression at a time. You're training people not to take you seriously.

Questions

How long does a brand build take?

Foundation packages deliver in 7–10 business days. Full Build is 10–21 days. Rush delivery is available. Timeline starts after your discovery questionnaire is complete.

How many concepts will I see?

Three distinct concepts exploring different creative directions. You choose one, and we refine it until it's exactly right — unlimited revisions included from that point.

Do I need this before getting a website?

Recommended. A Digital HQ built on a weak brand is still a weak brand. We can build both together, or start with The Blueprint first — the order matters.

I already have a logo. Can you work with it?

Maybe. We'll audit what you have honestly and tell you if it's worth refining or if starting fresh is the smarter move. No upsell — just a straight answer.

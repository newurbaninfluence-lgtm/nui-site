Frequently Asked
Questions.

How much does a brand identity package cost in Detroit?

The Blueprint Foundation starts at $2,500 and includes a complete brand identity system — logo suite, color palette, typography, brand voice, social media templates, and brand guidelines. Financing is available from $89/month with 0% interest through Afterpay and Klarna. The Full Build is $5,500 and includes additional collateral. The Grand Opening package starts at $12,500 and includes a Digital HQ build.

Is it worth hiring a branding agency instead of using Canva or AI?

Canva and AI tools give you a template. They don't give you a strategy. They don't know your neighborhood, your competitors, or why your best customers actually chose you. The Blueprint builds a system — not just a logo — that tells your story consistently across every touchpoint and makes every advertising dollar you spend work harder.

How long does brand identity design take?

The Blueprint Foundation typically completes in 7–14 business days. The Full Build takes 14–21 days. The process includes a discovery call, strategy session, initial concepts, two revision rounds, and final delivery with a brand guidelines walkthrough.

Can I pay for branding in installments?

Yes. New Urban Influence offers 0% interest financing through Afterpay (4 payments over 6 weeks) and Klarna (flexible monthly plans up to $10,000). You can also split payments 50/25/25 or choose 3 equal monthly installments directly with NUI. Financing is approved instantly at checkout — no paperwork required.

Do I need a website before I get branding done?

No — The Blueprint is a standalone service that doesn't require a Digital HQ first. In fact, the opposite is true: getting your brand built first means your website, social media, and every future marketing piece will all look cohesive from day one. Your Blueprint feeds directly into your Digital HQ build when you're ready.

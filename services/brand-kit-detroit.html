The Problem

A Weak Brand Costs You
Jobs Every Single Day.

People decide if they trust you in seconds — before they read a word, before they call, before they walk in. That decision is made based on how you look. A weak brand doesn't just miss the sale. It sends customers straight to your competitor.

You look like everyone else

Generic logo, random colors, no system — customers can't tell you apart from the five other businesses that do exactly what you do.

You can't charge what you're worth

Premium pricing requires a premium brand. A Canva logo keeps your price ceiling low before you ever open your mouth.

Inconsistency kills trust

Your website looks different from your card, which looks different from your Instagram. Every inconsistency is a signal that you're not serious.

No strategy behind the design

A logo is not a brand. A brand is a system built for your specific customer, your market, and your growth goals. Most businesses never get this right.

Questions

Common Questions.

How do I know which package I need?

Brand Kit = launching or refreshing on a focused budget. Service Brand = you sell your expertise and need to look premium on proposals and in person. Product Brand = you sell physical goods and need everything from label to shelf to social to work together. Not sure? Book the call — we'll tell you straight.

How long does it take?

Brand Kit: 7–10 business days. Service Brand Identity: 10–21 days. Product Brand Identity: 14–28 days. Rush delivery is available. Timeline starts after your discovery questionnaire is complete.

How many logo concepts will I see?

Three distinct concepts, each exploring a different creative direction. You choose one and we refine it until it's exactly right — unlimited revisions from that point forward.

I already have a logo. Can you work with it?

Maybe. We'll audit what you have honestly and tell you if it's worth refining or if starting fresh is the smarter call. No upsell — just a straight answer after seeing what you've got.

Do I need this before getting a Digital HQ?

Recommended. A Digital HQ built on a weak brand is still a weak brand. We can do both together through the Grand Opening package, or sequence them — brand first, then build the HQ on top of it.

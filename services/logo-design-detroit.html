The Real Cost of Logo Design in Detroit

Let's be real about pricing. You can get a logo for $50 on Fiverr or pay a big agency $25,000+. Here's what you're actually buying at each level:

At the $50-200 range, you're getting a template with your name slapped on it. No strategy, no research, no understanding of your market. These logos look like every other logo.

At the $1,500-4,500 range (where NUI sits), you're getting a strategic, hand-crafted logo with research, multiple concepts, revisions, and complete file delivery. This is the sweet spot for small businesses that want professional quality without enterprise pricing.

At $10,000+, you're paying for the agency's overhead — fancy office, account managers, project managers. The actual design work isn't dramatically different from what a focused boutique agency delivers.

Frequently Asked Questions

DETROIT FAQ

How much does logo design cost in Detroit?

Professional logo design in Detroit ranges from $1,500 to $10,000+ depending on the agency. At New Urban Influence, logo design starts at $1,500 as part of our Brand Kit package, which includes your primary logo, secondary mark, icon, color palette, typography, and brand guidelines. Payment plans are available.

How long does it take to design a logo?

Our logo design process takes 2-3 weeks from kickoff to final delivery. This includes a strategy session, 3 initial concepts, up to 2 rounds of revisions, and final file preparation. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

What files do I get with my logo?

You receive all source files including AI, EPS, SVG, PNG (transparent and on white), PDF, and JPG in both CMYK for print and RGB for digital. We also provide social media-optimized sizes and a favicon file.

Do you offer logo design without a full branding package?

Our Brand Kit ($1,500) is our most affordable option and includes logo design along with essential brand elements. We don't offer standalone logo design because a logo without a cohesive brand system is like a front door without a house — it doesn't tell the full story.

Can I use my NUI logo on merchandise and products?

Yes — you own full rights to your logo. Use it on merchandise, products, vehicles, signage, packaging, or anywhere else. We provide print-ready files specifically optimized for large format and product printing.

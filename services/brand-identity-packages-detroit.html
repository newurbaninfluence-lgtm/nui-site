Services & Details

Why Full Brand Identity (Not Just a Logo)

A logo by itself is like a front door without a house. It looks good on its own, but it can't do its job without the complete system around it. A full brand identity gives you consistency across every touchpoint — your website, business cards, social media, packaging, signage, and internal documents all look like they belong to the same professional business.

Consistent branding increases revenue by up to 23%. Customers trust businesses that look put-together. Investors take you seriously when your pitch deck matches your website matches your business card. A complete brand identity pays for itself.

Our Process: Strategy Before Design

Every package starts with a strategy session. We learn about your business goals, target audience, competitive landscape, and brand personality. Then we create a mood board and brand direction for your approval. Only after strategy is locked in do we begin design.

This process prevents the most common branding mistake: designing something that looks cool but doesn't connect with your actual customers. Strategy ensures your brand identity is built on a solid foundation, not just aesthetic preferences.

Which Package Is Right for You?

Choose the Brand Kit ($1,500) if you're just starting out and need professional fundamentals to launch. This gives you everything to look credible on social media, business cards, and basic marketing materials.

Choose Service Business Brand Identity ($4,500) if you're a consultant, agency, coach, or professional service provider who needs to look established and trustworthy. This package includes business collateral, presentation templates, and everything you need to pitch and close clients.

Choose Product Brand Identity ($5,500) if you sell physical products and need packaging, labels, and retail-ready brand assets. This package makes sure your product looks premium on shelves, on Amazon, and in customer hands.

BRAND IDENTITY FAQ

What's included in the $1,500 Brand Kit?

The Brand Kit includes: brand strategy session, mood board approval, primary logo with 3 initial concepts, secondary logo, icon mark, 5-color palette with hex/RGB/CMYK codes, 2-font typography selection, 15 social media Canva templates, brand guidelines document, and all source files in AI, EPS, SVG, PNG, and PDF formats. Payment plan: 2 payments of $750.

What's the difference between the three packages?

The Brand Kit ($1,500) covers the essentials — logo, colors, fonts, social templates, and guidelines. The Service Business tier ($4,500) adds business collateral — cards, letterhead, email signature, presentation template, expanded social kit, and comprehensive guidelines. The Product tier ($5,500) adds everything for physical products — packaging design, labels, dielines, product photography direction, and retail display mockups.

Can I upgrade from Brand Kit to a higher package later?

Yes — we offer upgrade paths for existing clients. If you start with the Brand Kit and later need packaging or business collateral, we apply a portion of your original investment toward the higher-tier package.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes — every package has a payment plan with no interest or fees. Brand Kit: 2 payments of $750. Service Business: 3 payments of $1,500. Product Business: 3 payments of $1,833. Work begins after the first payment.

How many revisions are included?

Brand Kit includes 2 rounds of revisions. Service and Product packages include 3 rounds. We present 3 initial logo concepts so you start with options, and revisions refine the chosen direction. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are available at $150/round.

How long does a full brand identity take?

Brand Kit: 2-3 weeks. Service Business Brand Identity: 3-4 weeks. Product Brand Identity: 4-5 weeks. Timeline depends on revision speed and content readiness on your end. Rush delivery available for an additional 25% fee.

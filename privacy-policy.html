Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

New Urban Influence ("NUI," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit newurbaninfluence.com, use our client portal, or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business name when you contact us, book a consultation, or register as a client.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and IP address — collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Communications: Messages, project briefs, and feedback submitted through our forms or email.
  • Visitor Identification Data: We use third-party tools including RB2B to identify business visitors for marketing follow-up purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver and manage our branding and design services
  • Respond to inquiries and provide client support
  • Send project updates, invoices, and service communications
  • Improve our website, tools, and service offerings
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Analyze website traffic and advertising campaign performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies including:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — website traffic and user behavior analysis
  • Google Tag Manager — tracking script management
  • Meta Pixel (ID: 711956342472188) — ad performance measurement and retargeting on Facebook/Instagram
  • Google Ads Pixel — conversion tracking from Google advertising
  • Web Push Notifications — browser-based updates with your explicit consent
  • RB2B — business visitor identification for B2B marketing outreach

You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Some site features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (Supabase, Netlify, Google, Meta, RB2B) solely to operate our business and deliver services. All third parties are contractually bound to keep your data confidential.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes in this policy or as required by law. Client project data is retained for a minimum of 3 years following project completion.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request data portability where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, email info@newurbaninfluence.com.

7. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data collected through our website or client portal, email info@newurbaninfluence.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure to protect your data. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies independently.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

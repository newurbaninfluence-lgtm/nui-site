Highland Park Business Branding

WHY HIGHLAND PARK BUSINESSES CHOOSE NEW URBAN INFLUENCE

Highland Park is a city with an outsized legacy. Home to Henry Ford's first moving assembly line and one of Michigan's most storied Black communities, Highland Park carries deep roots in American innovation and urban resilience. Nestled entirely within Detroit, it has its own identity, its own pride, and its own story.

Today, Highland Park is a city in creative renaissance — community organizations, artists, food entrepreneurs, and neighborhood builders are forging something new here. Your brand needs to honor that heritage while positioning you for where Highland Park is going. That's exactly what we do.
